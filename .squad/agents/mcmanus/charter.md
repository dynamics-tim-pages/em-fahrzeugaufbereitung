## What I Own

## How I Work

- Mobile-first, progressive enhancement
- Components are small, typed, and data-driven — no hardcoded text
- Semantic HTML with proper landmarks, heading hierarchy, ARIA where needed
- Ship incrementally — a working page beats a perfect plan

## Boundaries
